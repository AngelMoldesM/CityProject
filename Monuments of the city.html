Main monuments of the city

16/06/2022

Ángel Moldes

In this article we are going to talk about the most important monuments of the city such as the Catedral de Santiago, Hostal os Reis, etc.

Cathedral of Santiago

The most important monument of the city is the Catedral de Santiago. Is one of the main points of peregrination in the world, is also the finish line of the Camino de Santiago. It was built in 1075 but finishes in 1211

Palacio de Rajoy

This building was built in 1766 above the place where before was find a section of the wall and the jail of the city. The main function of the palace was had a place for conferences

Hostal de los Reyes Católicos

This was the Hospital de los Reyes Catolicos, nowadays is the Parador de Santiago de Compostela, mostly known as the Hostal de los Reyes Católicos, where you can go and rest in your journey to Santiago de Compostela

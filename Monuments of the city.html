Main monuments of the city

16/06/2022 Ángel Moldes

In this article we are going to talk about the most important monuments of the city such as the Catedral de Santiago, Hostal os Reis, etc.

Cathedral of Santiago

The most important monument of the city is the Catedral de Santiago. Is one of the main points of peregrination in the world, is also the finish line of the Camino de Santiago. It was built in 1075 but finishes in 1211. It is also among the remaining churches in the world built over the tomb of an apostle

Palacio de Rajoy

The Rajoy Palace is a building in Santiago de Compostela, Spain. It is located in the Plaza del Obradoiro. It is the seat of the City Council and the presidency of the Junta de Galicia.
It is a neoclassical style construction, its construction was ordered by the Archbishop of Santiago, Bartolomé Raxoi Losada, in 1766 with the purpose of serving as a seminary for confessors. The French engineer Carlos Lemaur was responsible for the work. On its cover you can see a representation of the battle of Clavijo and a sculpture of the apostle Santiago.
The palace is located in the western part of the Plaza del Obradoiro, which closes on that side, in front of the main façade of the Cathedral of Santiago de Compostela. To its right is the Colegio de San Jerónimo, which today serves as the headquarters of the Rectorate of the University of Santiago; To the left of the palace is the Royal Hospital of the Catholic Monarchs, currently a hostel.

Hostal de los Reyes Católicos

This was the Hospital de los Reyes Catolicos, nowadays is the Parador de Santiago de Compostela, mostly known as the Hostal de los Reyes Católicos, where you can go and rest in your journey to Santiago de Compostela
